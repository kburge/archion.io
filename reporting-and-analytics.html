Real Time Marketing Intelligence

With a deep background in business intelligence and data anlysis, we can help you create amazing marketing dashboards so that you know the state of your marketing campaigns at a glance.

If you are like a lot of companies, your data is stored in separate silos that are not connected, which makes it very difficult to get a 360 degree view of your marketing systems. 

In addition, what little data you can get access to is likely just historical, reporting on what happened last month, or if you are lucky last week. This backward looking analysis is helpful, but limited.

I liken this to driving through a winding forest road at 100 mph and trying to steer your car by looking through your rear view window!  

What is needed is real time performance data from your campaigns.  What is the ROI of my ad spend today?  What version of my ad is getting the highest click thru rate, right now?

The good news is that this level of tracking, reporting, and analysis is possible today if you have the right partner helping you connect your systems, normalize your data, and automate the daily update of a modern marketing dashboard.

We've been building best-of-breed executive dashboards for years, in fact, business intelligence is in our DNA.
